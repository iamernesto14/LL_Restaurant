About Us

Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant in Chicago, founded by Italian brothers Mario and Adrian. We blend Mediterranean traditions with a modern twist, offering a seasonal menu inspired by Italy, Greece, and Turkey. Mario, the chef, brings family recipes from Italy, while Adrian focuses on marketing and expanding the menu. The restaurant offers a relaxed atmosphere with reasonable prices, making it a popular dining spot throughout the day. Little Lemon is a place where culinary traditions and creativity converge to create a warm and welcoming dining experience.
